Bitcoin Technical Analysis - January 27, 2026

January 27, 2026

Current Situation: BTC is trading at $88,484 after correcting from its all-time high of ~$124,000. Price is currently consolidating between the 0.236 ($98,554) and 0.382 ($82,673) Fibonacci levels.

Key Levels to Watch:

  • Support: $82,673 (0.382 Fib) → $80,000 (trendline) → $69,838 (0.5 Fib)
  • Resistance: $98,554 (0.236 Fib) → $120,000 → $124,225 (ATH)

Scenario: If the $82-80K zone holds as support, we could see a move towards $98K-100K. However, a break below $80K could lead to a test of the $70K-$57K region.

Recent News: Institutional interest in Bitcoin continues with major banks expanding their crypto services. The upcoming Fed decisions remain a key catalyst for crypto markets.
